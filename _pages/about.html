Vision

To become a leading private research hub in developing innovative solutions for horticultural exports in Nigeria.

Mission

To research and develop innovative breeding, packaging, and supply chain solutions for horticultural crops.

Core Values

  • Integrity - Upholding ethical standards in all our research and business practices
  • Innovation - Pursuing creative solutions to tropical agricultural challenges
  • Collaboration - Working together across disciplines and with industry partners
  • Excellence - Striving for the highest quality in our research and outputs
  • Sustainability - Ensuring our solutions benefit people and the environment for generations

Core Competencies

We are a team of highly innovative researchers, each bringing specialized knowledge and a shared passion for advancing tropical agriculture. Our collective expertise spans the critical domains of food technology, material science, and quantitative genetics.
